Digital Detox

Publish date: 2020-12-12
Tags: Diary, English

Today is my digital detox day.

I’m going to turn of all my remote communication methods related to SOSAD.FUN, and have a good sleep, and eat well, and exercise, and be myself.


I don’t usually do this.

I made this decision 13h ago. It was 9am. I have not slept for another day or so. It was my typical day.

Suddenly, I felt my heart. By felt, I mean, I reasoned that something must have had went wrong with my heart. One never feel one’s body, unless part of it went wrong.

I panicked for a moment, and decided to detox myself from this website stuff.

I turned off all my remote communication where people can reach out to me for immediate help.

I had a 12h long sleep.

I woke up. The first thing I wanted to do was to reach out for my phone and check for messages. It was by that time I realized that, though the website related absolute work hours were not that much, it was a 24/7 on call position and qualifies to 2 full time jobs in the past 3 years.

I measured my blood pressure (I did not dare to do the measuring before this sleep). My DIA jumped to 92. The second time in a row this week that I had my DIA blood pressure above 90. One of my friend once joked about it: “Are you like my dad now? He is in his sixies and his doctor gave him medicine to prevent him from having blood pressure above 90.”

After digital detox for 13 hours, I realized how much I have been worn out.

For something that does not even cover my medical insurance, I guess that does not worth giving away my health with.
